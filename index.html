    About Us


    The Bloc Group Limited is a diversified and dynamic company with strategic interests in real estate, liquefied petroleum gas (LPG), international trade and environmental protection. With a commitment to excellence, we leverage innovation, market expertise and strategic partnerships to deliver high-value solutions across multiple industries.



     


    REAL ESTATE

    The Bloc Group Limited stands as a leading real estate development company in Nigeria, dedicated to redefining skylines and positively impacting communities. Our mission centres around delivering sustainable, innovative, and value-driven developments that not only meet but exceed modern expectations. We pride ourselves on crafting world-class real estate solutions meticulously tailored to address the distinctive needs of Nigeria. Our portfolio encompasses commercial, residential, and mixed-use projects, all designed to bridge the divide between contemporary infrastructure and sustainable urban living. Through thoughtful planning and execution, we strive to create environments that foster growth and well-being for occupants and the wider community alike. Our commitment lies in providing intelligent, environmentally friendly housing, cutting-edge commercial spaces, and advanced infrastructure. We offer a comprehensive range of services, including property development, management, real estate investment, and advisory. Whether delivering luxury residences or affordable housing options, our focus remains on quality, sustainability, and meeting our clients’ diverse requirements. Since our establishment, The Bloc Group Limited has earned a distinguished reputation for excellence, transparency, and the punctual delivery of projects. Our operations extend across key Nigerian cities, such as Lagos and Abuja, as well as other regions, reflecting our ambition to make a lasting difference throughout the nation.

    LIQUEFIED PETROLEUM GAS

    The Bloc Global Energy & Petroleum Limited, a subsidiary of The Bloc Group Limited, is a leading Liquefied Petroleum Gas (LPG) retail company dedicated to making clean energy accessible and affordable. We specialize in the distribution and retailing of LPG for domestic and commercial use. We cater to households, businesses, and industries with safe and reliable LPG solutions supported by a robust distribution network and a commitment to safety and sustainability.

    INTERNATIONAL TRADE

    The Bloc Group Limited specializes in the import, export, and distribution of high-quality products across diverse industries. We leverage a robust network of suppliers and buyers, ensuring efficient delivery and competitive pricing for our clients. Our focus areas include agricultural commodities, building materials, industrial raw materials, consumer goods and technology products. We serve as a vital link in global trade, empowering businesses and communities through reliable, ethical and innovative trading solutions.

    ENVIROMENTAL PROTECTION

    The Bloc Group Limited is deeply committed to environmental protection and forest conservation. We are spearheading some environmental initiatives and conservation projects within Nigeria. We are also exploring potential collaboration with the government specifically for forest conservation.
