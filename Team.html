Obum Igboanugo

Founder and Chief Executive Officer of The Bloc Group Limited
Obum Igboanugo

Obum Igboanugo is an accomplished Lawyer and a seasoned Entrepreneur with a wealth of experience across multiple industries including financial markets, real estate, international trade, oil and gas and technology. He is the founder and Chief Executive Officer of The Bloc Group Limited. He is an experienced professional capable of applying expertise to various industries and bringing invaluable insights and strategies to his businesses. He has a keen ability to identify market trends and leverage opportunities for growth. He has founded successful companies that focuses on sustainable investments, achieving impressive returns while promoting environmental responsibility. Recognizing the potential for global commerce, he has engaged in strategic partnerships that have facilitated several local and cross-border transactions. With a diverse portfolio and a proven track record of success, Obum examplifies the modern entrepreneur – adaptable, result driven, forward-thinking and dedicated to making a positive impact across multiple sectors. He has demonstrated a track record of achieving significant results. Beyond business, Obum is committed to philantropy, supporting education and environmental initiatives through his foundation – The Bloc Charity Foundation. He believes in giving back to the community and empowering the indigents in the society
